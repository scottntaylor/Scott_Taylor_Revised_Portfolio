About Me

Hello! My name is Scott Taylor and I am a web developer currently enrolled in the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp. Prior to my enrollment in the UW Coding Bootcamp, I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication with an emphasis on advertising from Washington State University. After graduation, I returned back to Seattle where I worked for a small business (Eastside Audio Video) in Bellevue as a Sales Associate and their Assistant Marketing Director. Following my time with Eastside Audio Video, I have been working for a Seattle based clothing company, Cutter & Buck. At Cutter & Buck I have worked in sales and account management within our wholesale divisions, specifically with golf, team, and specialty retail accounts.

After a few years in the workforce, I have decided to further my education and delve into the world of coding. Coding has always been an interest of mine and only recently have I decided that it is the best path for me to continue down. Within the world of coding I see opportunities to combine creativity, problem-solving and logic to develop innovative and useful applications. Additionally, with the changing landscape of the job market in the Seattle area, I feel strongly that coding is the future. On top of my coding experience, I also have experience working with a variety of Adobe applications such as InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop.

Moving forward, I am excited for the opportunities that a coding education will provide me. I am eager to create and develop solutions to problems. To find out more or to set up a meeting, please visit my contact page and leave me a note.

Let's create something together!
